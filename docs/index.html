SmartRing

A circular app launcher — press a hotkey, point your mouse, launch instantly.

环形应用启动器 — 按下快捷键唤出圆形菜单，移动鼠标选择，松开即启动。

Move mouse over the ring / 移动鼠标到圆环上
PyQt5Windows Python 3.7+MIT

Features

功能特性

Everything you need for quick app launching at your fingertips.

指尖轻触，应用即达。

⚡ Hotkey Summon

Press a configurable hotkey (default F12) to summon the ring anywhere. Supports modifiers: Ctrl, Alt, Shift, Win.

按下可配置快捷键（默认 F12）在屏幕任意位置唤出环形菜单。支持 Ctrl/Alt/Shift/Win 组合键。

🎯 Mouse Selection

Move your mouse toward the desired app — auto-highlight with icon enlargement and label pop. Release to launch.

移动鼠标到对应扇区自动高亮，图标放大 + 标签弹出。松开快捷键或点击即启动应用。

🎨 Customizable Look

8 preset accent colours + custom hex. Ring size, hub size, icon size, background colour — all adjustable with live preview.

8 种预设主题色 + 自定义色值。圆环外径、中心半径、图标大小、背景色均可调整，带实时预览。

🔄 Dual Trigger Mode

Hold mode: press & hold to show, release to launch. Toggle mode: press once to show, press again to hide.

Hold 模式：按住唤出、松开启动。Toggle 模式：按一下显示、再按隐藏。适配不同使用习惯。

🚀 Auto-Start

Highest-priority setting. Launch with Windows — always ready when you need it.

最高优先级设置项。随 Windows 自动启动，确保随时可用。可通过任务管理器管理。

⌨ Keyboard Visual

Built-in simulated keyboard in settings highlights captured hotkey keys. Conflict detection for 30+ system shortcuts.

设置面板内置模拟键盘，输入快捷键时自动高亮对应按键。30+ 系统快捷键冲突检测与提醒。

👁 Live Preview

Save settings then preview the real ring in a popup window. Adjust → Preview → Confirm — see exactly what you get.

保存设置后可弹出预览窗口查看真实圆环效果。调整 → 预览 → 确认，所见即所得。

🔌 Mouse Side Button

Map your mouse's side button to F12 via its driver software for the ultimate one-hand workflow.

通过鼠标驱动软件将侧键映射为 F12，单手操作，效率翻倍。
